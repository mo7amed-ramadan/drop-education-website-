About us

Drop is an online learning platform that offers courses in a wide range of subjects, from programming and data science to business and marketing. The platform is designed to help learners acquire new skills and knowledge at their own pace, with engaging and interactive content that includes videos, quizzes, and hands-on projects. Drop's courses are created by industry experts and delivered through intuitive and user-friendly interfaces, making it easy for learners of all levels to get started. In addition, the platform provides learners with personalized feedback and support from instructors and peers, empowering them to achieve their learning goals and advance their careers.
